Transforms & ROC

L. $X(s)=\int x(t)e^{-st}dt$. FT iff $j\omega\subset$ROC.

Z. $X(z)=\sum x[n]z^{-n}$. DTFT iff $|z|{=}1\subset$ROC.

ROC rules (both). No poles. Right: $\sigma{>}\sigma_{\max}$ / $|z|{>}r_{\max}$. Left: reverse. Two-sided: strip / annulus. Finite duration: all $s$ / all $z$ (maybe not $0,\infty$). Causal+stable $\Leftrightarrow$ LHP / $|p|{<}1$. BIBO stable $\Leftrightarrow$ $j\omega$-axis / unit circle $\subset$ ROC.

PFE

Distinct: $\sum A_k/(s{-}p_k)$, cover-up $A_k{=}(s{-}p_k)X|_{p_k}$.

Repeated: order $r$ at $p$: $\sum B_k/(s{-}p)^k$, $B_k{=}\tfrac{1}{(r-k)!}\tfrac{d^{r-k}}{ds^{r-k}}[(s{-}p)^rX]|_p$. Pair $\tfrac{1}{(s+a)^k}\leftrightarrow \tfrac{t^{k-1}}{(k-1)!}e^{-at}u$.

Complex conj.: $p{=}{-}a\pm j\omega_d$: complete square $(s{+}a)^2{+}\omega_d^2$, use cos/sin; don't split conjugates.

Z: always in $z^{-1}$. ROC picks right/left per pole. Improper: long-divide first.

Improper (both): $\deg{\rm num}\ge\deg{\rm den}$ $\Rightarrow$ long-divide. L: const $\to\delta(t)$; poly $\to\delta^{(k)}(t)$. Z: poly in $z^{-1}$ $\to$ finite $\delta[n{-}k]$.

CT System Analysis (Lec 18)

From ODE $\sum a_k y^{(k)}{=}\sum b_k x^{(k)}$, replace $d^k/dt^k\to s^k$: $H(s){=}\tfrac{\sum b_k s^k}{\sum a_k s^k}$.

Golden rule (CT): causal + BIBO stable $\Leftrightarrow$ all poles $\Re\{p_i\}{<}0$ (open LHP). Pole in RHP $\Rightarrow$ unstable. Pole on $j\omega$-axis, none in RHP $\Rightarrow$ marginally stable.

Blocks: series $H_1H_2$; parallel $H_1{+}H_2$; feedback $H/(1{+}GH)$ (neg), $H/(1{-}GH)$ (pos).

Unil L IVP: transform ODE w/ ICs (minus) $\to$ solve $Y$ $\to$ ZSR+ZIR $\to$ PFE $\to$ invert $\to$ verify $y(0^+)$, $y(\infty)$. Total = ZSR + ZIR.

IVT / FVT (Laplace)

$x(0^+){=}\lim_{s\to\infty}sX(s)$; $x(\infty){=}\lim_{s\to 0}sX(s)$.

FVT valid only if all poles of $sX(s)$ have $\Re\{p\}{<}0$ (open LHP). Otherwise the formula returns garbage.

Sign Trap

Unil. L: $-y(0^-)$. Unil. Z: $+y[-1]$. Opposite signs.

System Analysis

CT. $y^{(k)}{\to}s^kY{-}\sum s^{k-1-j}y^{(j)}(0^-)$. Solve $Y{=}Y_{\rm ZS}{+}Y_{\rm ZI}$; PFE; invert. $H(s){=}Y_{\rm ZS}/X$.

Sampling & Feedback

Thm. Recovery iff $\omega_s{>}2\omega_M$ strict. Reconstruct: LPF gain $T$; sinc interp $x_r{=}\sum x(nT)\,{\rm sinc}(\tfrac{t{-}nT}{T})$.

Aliasing irreversible. AAF before sampler. $x(t{-}t_0)$ keeps rate; $x(at)$ scales by $|a|$. BW product: $\le\omega_{M,1}{+}\omega_{M,2}$. ZOH, FOH: practical but inexact (sinc droop).

Feedback regimes. $|GH|{\gg}1 \Rightarrow Q{\approx}1/G$ (fdbk dominates); $|GH|{\ll}1 \Rightarrow Q{\approx}H$ (fdbk negligible); $GH{=}{-}1 \Rightarrow$ unstable.

CL poles = roots of $1{+}GH{=}0$. Nyquist: $N{=}Z{-}P$; OL-stable $\Rightarrow$ CL-stable iff no encirclement of $-1/K$.

PM: $180^\circ{+}\angle L$ at $|L|{=}1$. GM: ${-}|L|_{\rm dB}$ at $\angle L{=}{-}180^\circ$. Both $>0$: stable. $\tau_{\max}{=}{\rm PM}_{\rm rad}/\omega_{gc}$. 1st-order: GM $=\infty$.

Marginal stability: poles on $j\omega$-axis (CT) or unit circle (DT) $\Rightarrow$ not BIBO stable; impulse response doesn't decay.

Top Traps

Always state ROC. Repeated pole $n$: $n$ PFE terms. DT: $|a|{<}1$, not $a{<}1$. FVT invalid if unstable/marginal. GM from $|L|$ at $-180^\circ$; PM from $\angle L$ at 0 dB. Poles of $\tfrac{1}{1{-}az^{-1}}$ at $z{=}a$. PFE in Z: $z^{-1}$ only. Complex conj.: complete square, don't split. RHP zero $\ne$ unstable. Don't mix Hz and rad/s ($\omega{=}2\pi f$). Neg fdbk doesn't always stabilize (excess gain destabilizes).
