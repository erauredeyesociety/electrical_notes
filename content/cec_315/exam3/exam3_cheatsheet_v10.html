Transforms & ROC

L. $X(s)=\int x(t)e^{-st}dt$. FT iff $j\omega\subset$ROC.

Z. $X(z)=\sum x[n]z^{-n}$. DTFT iff $|z|{=}1\subset$ROC.

ROC rules (both). No poles. Right: $\sigma{>}\sigma_{\max}$ / $|z|{>}r_{\max}$. Left: reverse. Two-sided: strip / annulus. Causal+stable $\Leftrightarrow$ LHP / $|p|{<}1$.

PFE

Distinct: $\sum A_k/(s{-}p_k)$, $A_k{=}(s{-}p_k)X|_{p_k}$.

Repeated: order $r$ at $p$: $\sum B_k/(s{-}p)^k$, $B_k{=}\tfrac{1}{(r-k)!}\tfrac{d^{r-k}}{ds^{r-k}}[(s{-}p)^rX]|_p$.

Complex conj.: $p{=}{-}a\pm j\omega_d$: complete square $(s{+}a)^2{+}\omega_d^2$, use cos/sin.

Z: always in $z^{-1}$. ROC picks right/left per pole. Improper: long-divide first.

Sign Trap

Unil. L: $-y(0^-)$. Unil. Z: $+y[-1]$. Opposite.

Sampling & Feedback

Thm. Recovery iff $\omega_s{>}2\omega_M$ strict. Reconstruct: LPF gain $T$; sinc interp $x_r{=}\sum x(nT)\,{\rm sinc}(\tfrac{t{-}nT}{T})$.

Aliasing irreversible. AAF before sampler. $x(t{-}t_0)$ keeps rate; $x(at)$ scales by $|a|$. BW product: $\le\omega_{M,1}{+}\omega_{M,2}$.

Feedback. CL poles = roots of $1{+}GH{=}0$. Nyquist: $N{=}Z{-}P$; OL-stable $\Rightarrow$ CL-stable iff no encirclement of $-1$.

PM: $180^\circ{+}\angle L$ at $|L|{=}1$. GM: ${-}|L|_{\rm dB}$ at $\angle L{=}{-}180^\circ$. Both $>0$: stable. $\tau_{\max}{=}{\rm PM}_{\rm rad}/\omega_{gc}$. 1st-order: GM $=\infty$.

Top Traps

Always state ROC. Repeated pole $n$: $n$ PFE terms. DT: $|a|{<}1$, not $a{<}1$. FVT invalid if unstable/marginal. GM from $|L|$ at $-180^\circ$; PM from $\angle L$ at 0 dB. Poles of $\tfrac{1}{1{-}az^{-1}}$ at $z{=}a$. PFE in Z: $z^{-1}$ only. RHP zero $\ne$ unstable. Neg fdbk doesn't always stabilize (excess gain destabilizes).
