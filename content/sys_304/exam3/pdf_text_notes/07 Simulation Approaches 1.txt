Newsvendor Problem вАУ Formulation
пБ∞ Choose q to maximize expected profit per day.
   вЧЉ q too small вАУ sell out, miss $0.45 profit per paper.
   вЧЉ q too big вАУ have left over, scrap at a loss of $0.52 per
     paper.
пБ∞ Classic operations-research problem.
   вЧЉ Many versions, variants, extensions, applications.
   вЧЉ Much research on exact solution in certain cases.
   вЧЉ But easy to simulate, even in a spreadsheet.

пБ∞ Profit in a day, as a function of q:
   W(q) = r min (D, q) + s max (q вАУ D, 0) вАУ cq.
              Sales revenue        Scrap revenue           Cost
       пБ∞ W(q) is a random variable вАУ profit varies from day to day.

пБ∞ Maximize E(W(q)) over nonnegative integers q.
Newsvendor Problem вАУ Simulation
пБ∞ Set trial value of q, generate demand D, compute
  profit for that day.
  вЧЉ   Then repeat this for many days independently, average
      to estimate E(W(q)).

  вЧЉ   Try for a range of values of q.
пБ∞ Need to generate demand D = max (пГЂXпГє, 0).
  вЧЉ   So need to generate X ~ normal (пБ≠ = 135.7, пБ≥ = 27.1).
       пБ∞ (Much) ahead - generating random variates.

  вЧЉ   In this case, generate X     = пБЖвИТпА±пБ≠,пБ≥(U).

         distribution.

Single-Server Queue вАУ Setup
пБ∞ Like hand simulation, but:
   вЧЉ Interarrival times ~ exponential with mean 1/пБђ = 1.6 min.
   вЧЉ Service times ~ uniform on [a, b] = [0.27, 2.29] min.
   вЧЉ Stop when 50th waiting time in queue is observed.
        пБ∞ i.e., when 50th customer begins service, not exits system.

пБ∞ Watch waiting times in queue WQ1, WQ2, ..., WQ50.
   вЧЉ   Important вАУ not watching anything else, unlike before.
пБ∞ Si = service time of customer i.

  Wqi+1 = max (WQi + Si вАУ Ai, 0), i = 2, 3, ...
