Description

For your final project, your team will analyze the decision problem you previously defined using trade studies, decision analysis, and risk analysis methods learned in this class.

The goal is to support a clear, justified recommendation using structured analysis - not to build the most complex model.

Instructions

  • Work in your assigned team of 3

  • Use the same decision problem from your earlier assignment

  • Apply at least one method per team member:

    • Trade study

    • Decision analysis

    • Risk or uncertainty analysis

  • Integrate results into one final recommendation

What to Submit

  • One PDF per team

  • Maximum length: 8 pages (figures and tables included; appendices optional)

  • One submission per team

Required Sections

Your report must include the following sections (use headings):

  1. Problem Overview

    • Decision statement

    • Application area and context

    • Decision-maker

  2. Objectives and Constraints

    • Clearly stated objectives

    • Constraints and assumptions

  3. Alternatives

    • Description of all alternatives considered

  4. Trade Study Analysis

    • Criteria used

    • Method applied

    • Key trade-offs and results

  5. Decision Analysis

    • Method used

    • Model structure and assumptions

    • Recommended alternative

  6. Risk and Uncertainty Analysis

    • Key risks and uncertainties

    • Analysis approach

    • Impact on decision

  7. Final Recommendation

    • Final recommended alternative

    • Justification based on analyses

  8. Team Contributions

    • Brief description of each member's role

Grading

This project is graded on correct application of methods, clarity of reasoning, and quality of the final recommendation.

Rubric 

Criteria Points
