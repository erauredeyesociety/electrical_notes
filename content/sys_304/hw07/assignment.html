Question 1 – QFD Calculations (25 pts)

A delivery drone system has the following:

Customer Requirements (CRs)

  • Fast delivery (5)
  • Low cost (4)
  • Reliability (5)

Engineering Characteristics (ECs)

  • Battery capacity (E1)
  • Motor efficiency (E2)
  • Payload capacity (E3)

Relationship Matrix (9 = strong, 3 = medium, 1 = weak)

E1 E2 E3
Fast delivery 3 9 3
Low cost 9 3 3
Reliability 3 9 1

Tasks:
a) Compute technical importance weights for E1–E3
b) Normalize the weights
c) Identify the most important engineering characteristic
