\section*{Questions}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Monochromatic, coherent light is incident on a Young's experiment having slits separated by 0.27 mm. The screen is 2.2 m away and an experimenter notes that the \emph{m}\textsuperscript{th} order maximum is 2.5 mm from the center line. Calculate the wavelength of the light in nanometers if \emph{m} = 3. \hfill \textit{(1 pts, Numeric)}

  \item A flat sheet of glass (\emph{n}\textsubscript{glass} = 1.75) has a thin coating of material applied to its upper surface. If the refractive index of the coating is 1.53 what minimum thickness (in nm) should this coating be in order to be nonreflective to light of vacuum wavelength 543 nm? \hfill \textit{(1 pts, Numeric)}

\end{enumerate}
