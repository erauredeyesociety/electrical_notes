# PS 160 — Midterm 1 Knowledge Questions (blank)

**[30 points]**

## Fluids

**1. [2 points]** Write the definitions of density and pressure.

**2. [2 points]** Write the hydrostatic condition and its solution for constant density.

**3. [2 points]** Write the continuity equation and Bernoulli's equation.

## Oscillations

**4. [2 points]** Write the differential equation for simple harmonic motion and its solution.

**5. [3 points]** Write the formulas for the angular frequency of small oscillations of a mass-spring system, a simple pendulum, and a physical pendulum.

**6. [3 points]** Write the relations between the frequency, angular frequency, and period.

## Waves and Sound

**(a) [3 points]** Write the wave function for a right-going wave, and the definitions of wavenumber and wave speed.

**(b) [3 points]** Write the formulas for the speed of waves on a string, in a solid, and in a fluid.

**(c) [2 points]** Write the formulas for the frequencies and wavelengths of standing waves.

**(d) [2 points]** Write the formulas for the average power of waves on a string and the intensity of sound waves.

**(e) [2 points]** Write the formulas for the pressure amplitude of sound waves and the intensity-distance relationship.

**(f) [2 points]** Write the formulas for the sound intensity level and the reference intensity.

**(g) [2 points]** Write the formulas for the Doppler shifted frequency (source to the right of listener) and the beat frequency.
