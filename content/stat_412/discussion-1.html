Module 1 Discussion

Introductions and Reflections

Please complete this two-part discussion forum.

First, introduce yourself to your instructor and your classmates by compiling a brief biography, which includes the following information:

  • Where you live (not your specific address, but city, state, country, etc.)
  • The type of work you do, if employed
  • Your professional and educational objectives
  • Something you would like us to know about you and something that would surprise us
  • Your time zone

Next, review the set of Learning Outcomes (LOs) for the course as shown in the Course Syllabus, and answer the following questions in your post:

  • Which learning outcome(s) interest you the most and why?
  • Which learning outcome(s) do you feel will be most relevant in your field and profession?
