ACAS Xa combines a probalistic dynamics model to determine where the aircrafts may go and a probabilistic sensor 
model to account for noise/error in sensor measurements.
Based on these models and the state of the vehicle, the aircraft’s state can be approximated.


--- Page 21 ---
•
With TCAS II, a set of rules is used to 
determine given the states of ownship 
and intruder the type of traffic alert or 
resolution advisory to issue.
•
ACAS Xa uses a look-up table to 
determine possible actions to take 
given the state. 
o Considering the associated cost of the 
action, the system selects action with the 
lowest cost.
