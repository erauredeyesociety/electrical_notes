•
Enables 3-axis precision pointing
o Stores torque and moment by the wheel spinning causing counter-rotation of space 
vehicle because of conservation of momentum
o Produces torque and momentum
•
How it works?  To counter a rotation along one of the spacecraft’s axes, the 
reaction wheel…
o Spins counter to rotation along the wheel’s axes is aligned with the axes of the 
rotation.
o Reduces angular velocity to zero
•
Common configuration is three-axes with the units orthogonally placed.
•
Issue: High failure rate:
o Larger space vehicles can include three backup wheels.
o Smaller, SWAP limited, vehicles typically use a four-wheel configuration providing one 
backup-wheel (often misaligned with other wheels)
•
If the counter rotation of the reaction wheel becomes saturated:
o Torque in opposing direction causes wheel rotation to slow, which allows it to store 
additional momentum and torque.
•
External actuation (e.g. thruster or magnetic torquer)

Sun Sensors


--- Page 14 ---
•
Cosine Detectors (aka photocells) aka Coarse Sun Sensors
o
Current generated by cell is proportional to the cosine of the angle between the sensor boresight and 
the sun.
o
Multiple may be needed to cover full sky.
•
Different locations around spacecraft
o
Advantages
•
Inexpensive
•
Low mass
o
Disadvantages
•
Sensitive to albedo effects
•
Few degree accuracy
•
Requires ADC (analog to digital converter)
•
Albedo – measurement of reflectivity
o
Earth Albedo averages 30%, but varies dramatically based on surface conditions (e.g. snow/ice vs. 
forests)
o
LEO spacecraft have reduced accuracy up to 10 degrees (coarse sun sensor)
o
Glint of sun off of horizon or other spacecraft can impact some sun sensors’ performance
Types of sun sensors
Cosine Sun Sensor | Bradford Space


--- Page 15 ---
•
Quadrant detectors
o
Sunlight enters window with 2x2 array of photo diodes
o
Current output is a function of the sun's location within the boresight
o
Also sensitive to Albedo effect
•
Digital Sun Sensors
o
Narrow slit with photo diodes behind a "geometric coded bit mask."
o
Mask enables the light to form different measurements based upon the 
sun's angle
o
Amplified and thresholded signals are then used to derive the sun's angle 
with respect to the sensor
•
Sun Cameras
o
Determine sun position through camera image
o
Optics must be carefully considered due to the bright sunlight.
o
Finds centroid of sun in image.
o
Multiple apertures can increase accuracy
o
Advantages
•
High accuracy
•
Albedo rejection
