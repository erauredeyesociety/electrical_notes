Redundancy
•
To address the risk of failure, the network can use redundancy to mitigate the risk of system failure.  
•
The figure shows the addition of a backup or fallback bus controller and a fallback channel.
•
Terminals and monitors are connected to both the primary and secondary bus (channels).
•
If the primary bus or its controller fails, the system can identify the fault and switch to the fallback controller and 
bus.
Also, while not redundancy, it should be noted a systems using MIL-STD-1553 can utilize one 
or multiple buses.
